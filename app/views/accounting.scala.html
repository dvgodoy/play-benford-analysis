Accounting

You can analyze your accounting records with just a few steps:

  • submit a CSV file with values in the first column;
  • if your values have an associated hierarchical structure (for instance, departments and sub-departments in a company),
    you can test your accounting data for different levels of granularity:
    • include the structure of your data by inserting it in the columns that follow the values:
      • top-level in the left-most column
      • bottom-level in the right-most column
  • your file should look like this:
    10.35,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT A,SUB-DEPARTMENT A.1,...
    128.17,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT A,SUB-DEPARTMENT A.2,...
    8712.33,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT B,SUB-DEPARTMENT B.1,...
    ...

And when the upload is complete, click on Load Data and explore the results!
