Accounting

To have your accounting records Benford-analysed, please submit a CSV file containing the values in the first column.
If your values have an associated hierarchical structure (for instance, departments and sub-departments in a company), you can take advantage of the drill-down capability, that is, you can test your accounting data for different levels of granularity.
To include the structure of your data, insert it in the columns that follow the values, from top-level in the left-most column to bottom-level in the right-most column.

Your file should look like this:

10.35,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT A,SUB-DEPARTMENT A.1,...
128.17,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT A,SUB-DEPARTMENT A.2,...
8712.33,COMPANY NAME,DEPARTMENT B,SUB-DEPARTMENT B.1,...
...

When the upload is complete, click on Load Data and explore the results!
